# Cloud & AI Startup Incentive Programs (2024-2025)

## Overview
Major cloud providers and AI platform companies offer significant startup programs that can substantially reduce infrastructure costs for biotech/pharma AI companies. These programs are especially valuable for genomics and digital twin applications requiring GPU compute.

---

## AWS Activate

### Standard Program
**Eligibility**: Pre-Series B startups, founded within last 10 years, with a company website.

**Benefits**:
- **With Accelerator/VC Partnership**: Up to **$100,000 USD** in AWS credits
- **Self-Funded Startups**: Up to **$1,000 USD** in credits
- Technical support, training, and educational content
- Access to AWS experts

**Application**: Through associated Activate Provider (VC, accelerator) or direct application

### 2025 Generative AI Accelerator
**Eligibility**: Highly selective - 40 startups chosen globally

**Benefits for Selected Companies**:
- **Up to $1,000,000 USD** in AWS credits
- Personalized mentorship from AWS AI/ML experts
- Access to curated support network and investor connections
- Priority focus on biotech/pharma use cases (e.g., pharmaceutical manufacturing optimization, drug discovery)

**Example Companies**: Basetwo AI (pharma manufacturing), Chai Discovery (AI drug discovery)

---

## NVIDIA Inception

### Core Program
**Eligibility**: Cutting-edge AI startups across all stages

**Benefits**:
- **10,000 ai.nvidia.com inference credits**
- Preferential pricing on **NVIDIA AI Enterprise** (includes Clara for healthcare/life sciences)
- Early access to NVIDIA healthcare products
- Access to **BioNeMo** platform for drug discovery
- Connection to VC Alliance and Healthcare Partners

### NVIDIA + Microsoft Partnership (2024-2025)
**Additional Benefits for Healthcare/Life Sciences Startups**:
- **Up to $150,000 USD** in Microsoft Azure credits
- AI development software and expert support
- Access to both NVIDIA and Microsoft ecosystems

**Example Companies**: Nanyang Biologics (chronic disease drug discovery), VEIL.AI (privacy-preserving healthcare AI)

---

## Google for Startups Cloud Program

### AI-First Startups Track
**Eligibility**: Startups with AI/ML as core technology

**Benefits**:
- **Up to $350,000 USD** in Google Cloud credits over 2 years
- Dedicated AI training and webinars
- Access to Google Cloud AI/ML products
- Technical support from Google teams

### Standard Tracks
**Early-Stage (Pre-Funded)**:
- Up to **$2,000 USD** in credits

**Funded Startups (Pre-Seed to Series A)**:
- Year 1: Up to **$100,000 USD**
- Year 2: Additional 20% coverage (up to **$100,000 USD**)

### 2025 Cloud AI Accelerator
**Eligibility**: High-potential, deeply technical AI-first startups

**Benefits**:
- 10-week intensive program
- Equity-free support
- Dedicated mentorship from Google AI teams
- Product credits and expert network access
